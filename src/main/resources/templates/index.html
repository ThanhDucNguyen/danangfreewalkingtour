1. Han Market

20 Mins Walk
It is opened during the French
It’s the humming heart of the busy city
The Greatest place for finding some great bargains on souvenirs, clothes and local foods
The Han market is opened daily from 7:00 am - 7:00 pm.

2. Cathedral

2 Mins Walk
Da Nang Cathedral was built by French priest Louis Vallet in 1923 and covered pink-painted edifice
The name comes from the rooster that sits atop the impressive steeple
The Cathedral is opened Monday to Saturday 5:00 am-7:30 pm, Sunday 5:00 am - 7:00 pm.

3. Cham Museum

10 Mins Walk
This old sandstone building house was established in 1919
It is an invaluable collection of more than 1500 objects about Cham culture, religion, and history
The museum is opened daily from 7:00 am to 5:30 pm.

4. Dragon Bridge

6 Mins Walk
One of the most unique and impressive architecture in Viet Nam
It is up to 2,500 smart LED lights system
The capability to “breathe” fire or water every Saturday and Sunday or on special occasions from 9:00 pm - 9.30 pm.

5. Han Bridge

2 Mins Walk
This is the first swing bridge designed and constructed by Vietnamese engineers
The Han River Bridge – the symbol for new vitality and the developing desire of the City
The capable of movement rotate 90 degrees in the middle part at 1:00 am.
